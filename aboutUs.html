About Us


"Vishwash Packages" is a leading Manufacturer and Supplier of Packaging Boxes. Located in the business hub Ludhiana, the company is owned and managed by Mr. Gurvinder Singh Khurmi and Mr. Vishwash Singh.







Infrastructure


We have a developed and well-built infrastructure which consists of established factory. We are equipped with all the latest and sophisticated machinery which helps us in meeting the increasing market demands. For testing the products we have advanced Testing Facilities which ensures the quality.


Team


We have a large team that is continuously involved in serving the clients well. Some of our team members have been with us since the start of the company. We also have quality controllers in our team who follow strict quality control measures to ensure that a product of high quality is delivered.


Client Satisfaction


Satisfaction of our esteemed customers has always been our main objective. We believe that keeping the clients happy is our key to success and also our highest duty. For this we ensure delivering high quality products in a reasonable price limit within the expected time.
